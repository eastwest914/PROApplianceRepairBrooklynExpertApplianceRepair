FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our appliance repair services in Brooklyn, NY.

What types of appliances do you repair?

We repair a wide range of home and commercial appliances including refrigerators, washers, dryers, dishwashers, ovens, ranges, stoves, microwaves, freezers and more. We service all major brands including GE, Samsung, LG, Whirlpool, Bosch, Maytag, Kenmore, Frigidaire and many others.

How quickly can you come for a repair?

We offer same-day and next-day appointments throughout the week. Our technicians are available Monday through Sunday from 8am to 6pm. We understand that a malfunctioning appliance impacts your daily routine, and we do our best to respond as quickly as possible.

Do you charge a diagnostic fee?

We charge a service call fee which covers the visit and diagnosis. This fee is typically applied toward the total cost of the repair if you choose to proceed. We always provide a clear, upfront quote before beginning any repair work — no surprises on your final bill.

Do you service areas outside Brooklyn?

Yes, while Brooklyn is our home base, we also serve customers throughout Manhattan, Queens, the Bronx and Staten Island. Our Rockaway Pkwy location allows us to reach customers across the entire NYC metro area quickly and efficiently.

Is it worth repairing my appliance or should I replace it?

In most cases, repair is significantly more cost-effective than replacement, especially for appliances less than 10 years old. Our technicians will give you an honest assessment — if the repair cost approaches the value of the appliance, we will tell you so. We never recommend repairs that don't make financial sense for our customers.
